- **Ontario**
  - Economic powerhouse with a strong job market in finance, technology, and healthcare.
  - Average cost of living: $3,500 - $4,500/month.
  - Average salary: $60,000 - $70,000.
  - PR application success rate: 85%.
  - Cultural diversity and excellent educational institutions.
  - [Source: GetGIS](https://getgis.org/blog/which-province-in-canada-is-best-for-pr)

  - Average cost of living: $3,200 - $4,200/month.
  - Average salary: $55,000 - $65,000.
  - PR application success rate: 83%.
  - Offers excellent educational opportunities and leisure activities.
  - [Source: GetGIS](https://getgis.org/blog/which-province-in-canada-is-best-for-pr)
  
- **Quebec**
  - Culturally rich with good job opportunities and affordable living.
  - Average cost of living: $3,000 - $4,000/month.
  - PR application success rate: 81%.
